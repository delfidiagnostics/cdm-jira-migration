CDM Taxonomy — at a glance

Every ticket answers 4 questions: which Epic (corporate goal), what kind of work, on which project, for which study.

Hierarchy

Level 1 EEpic A 2026 corporate goal — or Departmental Ops — or Pre-2026 Legacy
Level 2 TTask A deliverable: a feature, a DQR listing, a refactor, a report, a service activity
Level 3 SSubtask A unit of work to deliver the Task

Epics — 6 total (4 corporate-goal-aligned + 2 special)

E1. CASCADE (L201) Readout
Successful readout of CASCADE (L201) primary performance using locked IVD test by 2H 2026
E2. IVD Lung PMA Submission
Submission-ready PMA package — clinical, analytical, manufacturing
E3. 4ITLR Readout
Successful readout of 4ITLR by July; conference presentation in 2026
E4. Reimbursement & Clinical Evidence
CU readout by January; CED Highmark Q4; two major clinical evidence publications Q2
E5. Departmental Ops
Service work, BAU, infra, recurring metrics — anything not tied to a 2026 corporate goal
E6. Pre-2026 Legacy
Pure archive — all pre-2026 tickets land here, no live work

Labels — three small prefixed namespaces

cat-*   (kind of work)

Exactly 1 per issue
cat-programming cat-reporting cat-operations

proj-*   (project / system)

1 or more per issue
proj-cpt proj-dqr proj-edc proj-metrics proj-cancer-review proj-pipeline proj-cro proj-sample proj-data-release proj-cv proj-av

study-*   (study)

Exactly 1 per issue
study-l101 study-l201 study-l301 study-4itlr study-onom study-cross
cat-* proj-* study-*

Statuses

To Do In Progress Completed Ongoing Cancelled

Ongoing is for recurring service work that never naturally closes (monthly CDR refreshes, scheduled metric reports). Cancelled is distinct from Completed so they don't blur in reporting.

Workflow note: the Jira workflow uses the existing names Done ≡ Completed and Dismissed ≡ Cancelled (we chose not to rename — semantically identical). Ongoing was added as a new workflow status on TESTCDM; on production CDM it maps to In Progress until added there too.
